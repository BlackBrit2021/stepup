About StepUp

The Core Problem

The problem with AI adoption is not the technology. It is that nobody teaches people how to use it with their own work. StepUp does not give you another AI tool. It gives you a coach that sits beside you and teaches you how to use the AI tools you already have.

Fear & Reluctance

StepUp is "learn by doing", not "learn then do". There is no course to complete before you start. You bring a real task, you do it with AI coaching, and you learn the skill in the process.

Our Mission

To eliminate the intimidation factor of modern technology and make AI accessible, understandable, and practically useful for everyone, regardless of their starting technical proficiency.
