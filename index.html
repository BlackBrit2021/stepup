How AI Coach Works

A coaching platform that closes the gap between having AI tools and knowing how to use them — by guiding you through your actual work, one session at a time.

1

Complete Your VARK Assessment

Before anything else, you complete the VARK learning style questionnaire. The AI Coach uses your result to adapt how it delivers feedback — visually, verbally, through text, or hands-on tasks — so you absorb information faster and with less frustration.

2

Choose Your Track

Select from two tracks: "Unemployment & Life Skills" for job seekers covering CV writing, interview prep, LinkedIn, budgeting, and confidence — or "Workplace & Upskilling" for employees covering AI tools, Excel, Python, Power BI, and soft skills. Not a generic course: you choose what matters to your situation.

Growth Analytics tracks your progress without surveys or self-assessment. Time saved, confidence scores, task accuracy, and AI independence are all measured automatically. Before-and-after charts let you see exactly how far you've come since day one.

About AI Coach

A coaching platform that closes the gap — guiding people through real tasks, building real confidence, and measuring real progress.

The Gap

Most people have access to AI tools. Almost nobody has been taught how to use them with their own work. That gap — between having the tool and knowing what to do with it — is exactly what AI Coach closes.

The Solution

AI Coach does not give you another course to watch. It gives you a coach that sits beside you while you do your actual work — guiding you through real tasks, building real confidence, and measuring real progress.

Every session uses something you need to do anyway: a CV that needs improving, a report that needs writing, a spreadsheet that needs automating. You finish with a real output and a skill you can use again tomorrow.

Why "Doing" is the Point

Watching someone do a task teaches you the theory. Doing the task yourself — with a coach beside you explaining each step — builds the skill. AI Coach is built on that distinction. The learning happens inside the doing, not before it.

Our Mission

To close the gap — for every person who has AI tools they don't know how to use. To give them a coach that sits beside them, guides them through their real work, builds their genuine confidence, and proves their progress with data.
